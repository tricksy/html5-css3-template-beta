FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I get started?
Sign up for a free account and follow our quick-start guide. You'll be up and running in under five minutes. No credit card required.
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. You can explore every feature before committing. No credit card needed to start.
What kind of support do you offer?
Free plans get community support. Pro plans include priority email support with a 24-hour response time. Enterprise plans get a dedicated account manager and phone support.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You can cancel your subscription at any time and continue using the service until the end of your billing period.
